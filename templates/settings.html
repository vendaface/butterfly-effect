Privacy & Disclaimer

Unofficial tool — not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Monarch Money, Inc. Use at your own risk. Support comes from the individual creator, not from Monarch.

Your data never leaves this device
No Monarch credentials are stored on this computer. Only API keys for AI providers are stored in .env. The app connects to only two destinations on your behalf:
  • Monarch's servers — to fetch your account data, using your own login session
  • Your AI provider's API — only if you enable AI Insights and add an API key
No data is sent to any server operated by this tool or its author.
How authentication works
When you connect to Monarch, a real Chrome browser window opens on your computer and you log in directly on Monarch's website. Your password is never parsed or stored in memory beyond what Monarch's own login form uses. After a successful login, the session cookie is saved to browser_state.json on disk for future transaction retrieval.
AI provider terms
If AI Insights are enabled, a summary of your recent transactions is sent to your chosen provider. Review their privacy policy before enabling: Anthropic · OpenAI · Google
